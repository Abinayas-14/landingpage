ABOUT


Online courses, also known as e-learning or distance learning, refer to educational programs delivered over the internet. These courses are designed to provide instruction, training, or learning experiences through digital platforms. They can cover a wide range of subjects and are typically accessible to learners of all ages. Online courses can be self-paced or instructor-led, and they often incorporate multimedia elements like videos, interactive quizzes, discussion forums, and assignments.
